### Event Details:
- Date: April 4th, 2026
- Time: 8 AM - Midnight ET
- Location: Remote
- Silliness: Extreme

## My Experience
Although a lot of teams had multiple players, up to 10+, I played solo for this single-day CTF, competing against hundreds of other teams in the Open and Academic sections. I had a few hours to tackle some of the challenges, but not enough time to get to as many as I had hoped. I wish it would have lasted longer so I could have completed more of the challenges, but I enjoyed participating in this fun and humorous CTF. I'm glad I was able to learn some new things, test my skills, and gain more experience and knowledge. I realize I have a LOT more to learn, yet I am happy with the progress I have made thus far in my cybersecurity journey.
